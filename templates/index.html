Join us at KRC to embark on a journey of academic exploration and contribute to the vibrant research culture at KIMEP University.

What is NovaPro?

"Nova Prospectus" is a scientific digital journal created by KIMEP University students, for students, that showcases cutting-edge research and thought-provoking science articles from the next generation of future scientists and scholars.

The journal's mission is to provide undergraduate and graduate students a platform to disseminate their findings, share insights, and foster interdisciplinary collaboration.
