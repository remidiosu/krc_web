About Us

Who are we?

We are a student organization - “KIMEP Research Club” (KRC). The main goal of KRC is to engage KIMEP students in the scientific research community and help them develop their paper-writing skills. The organization also aims to promote students' works by publishing them in our scientific journal every semester.  At KRC, we strive to:

Develop Research Skills: Provide resources and guidance to help students enhance their research and paper-writing abilities.
Academic Excellence: Encourage the production of high-quality research papers through workshops, seminars, and mentorship programs.
Publish Student Works: Showcase the scholarly achievements of our members by publishing their research in our esteemed scientific journal every semester.

Join us at KRC to embark on a journey of academic exploration and contribute to the vibrant research culture at KIMEP University.

What is NovaPro?

What is NovaPro? "Nova Prospectus" is a scientific digital journal created by KIMEP University students, for students, that showcases cutting-edge research and thought-provoking science articles from the next generation of future scientists and scholars.

The journal's mission is to provide undergraduate and graduate students a platform to disseminate their findings, share insights, and foster interdisciplinary collaboration.
